Cirqle Ltd ("Cirqle", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use cirqle.co.uk or our services.

1. What we collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account data: name, email address, and password (hashed).
  • Transaction data: purchases tracked through our links, cashback amounts, withdrawal history.
  • Social post data: post URLs or screenshots you submit for cashback verification.
  • Device & usage data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent — via cookies and analytics tools.
  • Payment data: bank account or PayPal details for withdrawals. We do not store full card numbers.

2. How we use your data

We use your data to:

  • Create and manage your Cirqle account.
  • Track purchases and calculate cashback owed to you.
  • Verify social posts and process cashback payments.
  • Send you transactional emails (cashback confirmed, withdrawal processed, etc.).
  • Improve our platform through anonymised analytics.
  • Comply with legal obligations, including fraud prevention and financial regulations.

We will only send you marketing emails if you have opted in. You can unsubscribe at any time.

3. Sharing with third parties

We share data with:

  • Brand partners: purchase data (not your identity) to confirm cashback eligibility.
  • Payment processors: to process withdrawals securely.
  • Analytics providers: anonymised usage data only.

We never sell your personal data. We do not share your data with advertisers for targeting purposes.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to keep you signed in, track purchases, and understand how people use our site. See our Cookie Policy for details, or manage your preferences in your browser settings.

5. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Object to or restrict certain uses of your data.
  • Request a portable copy of your data.
  • Lodge a complaint with the ICO (ico.org.uk).

To exercise any right, email privacy@cirqle.co.uk. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Data retention

We keep your account data for as long as your account is active, plus 7 years for legal and financial compliance. Transaction records are kept for 7 years in line with HMRC requirements. You can request deletion of your account at any time.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including TLS encryption, password hashing, and regular security audits. Despite these measures, no system is 100% secure — please use a strong, unique password for your Cirqle account.

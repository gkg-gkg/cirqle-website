Getting started

How does Cirqle work?▼
Sign up for free, browse 500+ brand deals, click through to shop, then post about your purchase on social media. Once your post is verified, cashback is added to your wallet.
Is Cirqle free to use?▼
Completely free. There are no subscriptions, no membership fees. Cirqle earns a commission from brands when you shop — a portion of that comes back to you as cashback.
Which brands are on Cirqle?▼
We partner with 500+ brands across fashion, food, travel, tech, home and more — including Nike, ASOS, Gymshark, Apple, Booking.com, Deliveroo and many others. New brands are added every week.

Cashback

When do I receive my cashback?▼
Cashback typically appears in your wallet within 7–14 days after your post is verified. Some brands take up to 30 days to confirm the purchase.
Why is my cashback showing as "pending"?▼
Pending means we're waiting for the brand to confirm your purchase. This usually takes 7–14 days. Once confirmed, cashback moves to "available" in your wallet.
Can I use discount codes alongside Cirqle?▼
Generally no — using another discount code or going through another cashback platform can break our tracking link. Check each deal's terms as some brands do allow it.
What happens if I return an item?▼
If you return part or all of your order, cashback for the returned items will be removed from your wallet. If the cashback has already been withdrawn, we'll deduct it from a future transaction.

Withdrawals

How do I withdraw my cashback?▼
Go to your dashboard and click "Withdraw". You can transfer to your bank account or PayPal. Transfers usually arrive within 1–2 business days.
Is there a minimum withdrawal amount?▼
The minimum withdrawal is £1.00. There's no maximum — withdraw as much as you have available.

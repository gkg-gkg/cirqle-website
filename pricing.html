Common questions

Why is it free for me?▼
Brands pay us a commission when you shop through our links — it's the same affiliate model that powers most price comparison sites. We share that commission with you rather than keeping it all ourselves.
Are there any hidden fees?▼
None at all. No account fee, no withdrawal fee, no inactivity fee. The £0 is genuinely £0.
Is there a premium tier?▼
Not right now. We believe cashback should be accessible to everyone, not gatekept behind a subscription. We may introduce optional features in future, but the core cashback experience will always be free.
Do I have to post on social to earn?▼
Yes — the social post is how we verify your purchase and it's what brands pay for. The post doesn't need to be fancy, just genuine evidence of your shop.

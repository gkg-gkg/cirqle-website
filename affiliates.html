FAQ

When do I get paid?
Commission is credited to your wallet monthly, on the 1st. Minimum payout is £5.
Is there a limit on how many people I can refer?
No limit at all. Refer as many people as you like — you earn on every single one.
What counts as an "active referral"?
A referral is active if they've earned cashback at least once in the past 90 days.
Can I use my own referral link?
No — self-referral isn't allowed. Your referral link is only valid for genuinely new accounts.
